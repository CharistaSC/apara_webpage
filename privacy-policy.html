This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our website www.apara.asia. We are committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with international data protection laws including the GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and LGPD (Brazil).

We use your information solely for:

4. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, LGPD)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the email provided below.
